# Target Job Description

**Role:** Software Developer Intern (Remote)

## About the Role
We are looking for a motivated Software Developer Intern to join our team. This internship is ideal for students pursuing a degree in Computer Science or a related field who are eager to gain hands-on experience in software development and modern technologies.

## Responsibilities
- Develop, test, and maintain web applications.
- Write clean, efficient, and well-documented code.
- Collaborate with team members on software projects.
- Debug and troubleshoot technical issues.
- Participate in code reviews and team discussions.
- Learn and apply industry best practices.

## Requirements
- Currently pursuing a B.Tech/B.E. in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.
- Basic knowledge of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and programming fundamentals.
- Familiarity with Git and GitHub.
- Understanding of data structures and algorithms.
- Strong problem-solving and communication skills.
- Eagerness to learn new technologies.

## Preferred Skills
- Knowledge of React.js, Node.js, or Python.
- Experience with databases such as MySQL or MongoDB.
- Understanding of REST APIs.

## Benefits
- Hands-on industry experience.
- Mentorship from experienced developers.
- Flexible remote work environment.
- Opportunity to work on real-world projects.
